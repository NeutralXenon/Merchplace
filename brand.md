# Brand — Merchplace

Status: active

Direction: Gallery-editorial marketplace with warm-monochrome discipline.

Merchplace should feel like a collector desk for Solana event artifacts, not a generic crypto landing page. The interface is dark-native, image-forward, restrained, and practical. Solana color is used as a small proof-of-chain signal and action accent, not as a decorative wash.

Palette:
- Base: warm near-black charcoal
- Surface: ink and graphite panels
- Border: soft warm gray
- Text: soft ivory primary, muted pewter secondary
- Accent: restrained violet for primary actions
- Success: muted mint for escrow/protection signals
- Info: USDC blue only where currency is named

Typography:
- Sans: Inter, used with tighter hierarchy and fewer oversized headlines
- Numeric: system monospace for prices and marketplace terms

Voice:
- Specific, marketplace-native, collector-oriented.
- Avoid generic crypto hype like "powered by", "reimagined", and "future of".
- Prefer concrete terms: listing, escrow, receipt, shipping, drop, event, buyer protection.
